Pricing

You can hire me for the local and international photo shoots, sessions or other photography services. The prices can vary depending on the full day or half day order and on your event’s location.



You can choose my hourly, full day, or half day rates depending on your budget. Either of the rates includes an initial consultation, the discussion of what you expect and your requirements, the shooting process, image editing, production, and final product delivery.
